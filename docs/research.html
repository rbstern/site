Research groups

Group Goal
Develop applications of statistics to the law.
Study inductive statistical methods, with special interest in foundational issues.
Develop the new mathematics needed to construct a theory of the brain accounting for the experimental data gathered by neuroscience research.
Combine statistics and machine learning to create improved solutions to relevant real-world problems.
